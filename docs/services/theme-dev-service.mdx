## Hire a Developer

If you are looking for a custom theme for your website, Join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/Hzfj27k) to find/hire a developer to create a custom theme for you.

## Develop yourself

If you are a developer and want to develop a custom theme for your website, Check out the [Theme Development](../development/develop-custom-themes) section to get started.

## Provide Service

If you are a developer, you can join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/Hzfj27k) and apply for the `Theme Developer` role to provide development services to the community for a fee.
